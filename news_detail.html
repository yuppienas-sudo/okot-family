Family Reunion 2026 Announced

By Wilfred Okot • March 12, 2026

The Okot family is excited to announce the 2026 family reunion in Gulu, Uganda. Family members from all around the world are invited to join for celebrations, storytelling, and cultural performances.

What to expect

  • Traditional dances and music
  • Family history sessions
  • Community meals and reunion displays
  • Opportunities to connect with relatives across generations

For more details, please contact the family historian via email at wilfredokot55@gmail.com.
