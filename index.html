Welcome to the Okot Family Website

The Okot family traces its roots deep within the rich cultural heritage of Northern Uganda. For generations, we have stood together as a pillar of strength, unity, and tradition.

This website serves as a digital gathering place for all Okot family members worldwide — a place to share our stories, preserve our history, and strengthen the bonds that unite us.
